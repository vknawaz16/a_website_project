About Us

A Sanctuary of Peace in the Heart of Portland

Founded in 2009, Serenity Wellness Spa has been Portland's trusted destination for holistic wellness. We believe that true beauty and health emerge from inner balance, and our mission is to guide every guest on their journey to complete well-being.

Our certified therapists combine time-honored techniques with modern therapeutic approaches, using only organic, ethically sourced products. Every treatment is tailored to your unique needs, ensuring a personalized experience that nurtures both body and mind.

Our Mission

To create a serene environment where every individual can escape the stresses of daily life, reconnect with their inner self, and emerge feeling renewed, refreshed, and truly serene.

Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

Upon arrival, you will be greeted by our reception team and offered a warm herbal tea. We recommend arriving 15 minutes early to complete a brief wellness questionnaire. Your therapist will then consult with you to understand your goals and any health considerations before your treatment begins. After your session, you can relax in our serenity lounge with infused water and light refreshments.

We recommend booking 3-5 days in advance for standard treatments and 1-2 weeks ahead for weekend appointments or packages. However, we do accommodate same-day bookings when availability allows. Our online booking system is available 24/7, and you can also call us directly for last-minute inquiries.

We kindly ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made within 24 hours may be subject to a 50% charge. No-shows are charged the full appointment fee. We understand that emergencies happen, so please contact us as soon as possible if you need to change your appointment.
